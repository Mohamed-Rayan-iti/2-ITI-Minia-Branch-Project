ITI  Intensive Training Programs

The Intensive Training Programs aim to maximize awareness and knowledge outreach and to promote the ICT culture across Egypt and its remote suburbs.

Program Goals & Career Training Targets

Different IT Specializations

Developing a base of professional IT trainers capable of implementing technology transfer programs in different IT specializations.

Latest Technology

Boosting university graduates with the latest information technology trends to accelerate their engagement in the IT labor market.

Advancing Productivity

Advancing the productivity and efficiency of IT users in various businesses for work automation, promotion and facilitation.

Enriching Undergraduates Knowledge

Enriching the knowledge of University undergraduates in niche IT areas of high market demand.

Professional IT Trainers

Professional IT trainers to enrich the training sector and promote the culture of information technology across Egypt.

Creative Young Cadres

Creative young cadres especially in niche areas of high demand in the ICT market.

Distinguished IT Business Users

Distinguished IT business users especially in small and micro enterprises using IT tools for work automation, work promotion and facilitation.

"Prepare for career"
Bundle for ( Undergraduates )

for this segment we provide hands on training on the technologies in demand in the local. This training is delivered through our Prepare for Career Programs.

"Learn and Teach"
Bundle for ( University Staff / Trainers )

for this segment we provide customized hands on training programs on the latest technologies in their respective fields. This training is delivered through our Learn and Teach Programs.

"Nano Degrees"
Bundle for ( Fresh Graduates in IT Market Hot Spots )

for this segment we provide state of the art training on technologies that are in demanded on the global level. This training is delivered through our Nano-Degree Programs.

"Jump Start"
Bundle for ( Fresh Graduates in rural / remote locations )

for this segment we provide hands on training on the best ways to use technology in their work. Also, this training seeks to enhance their knowledge and develop their technological abilities in order to find better jobs.

"Business Boost"
Bundle for (Community Segments using IT in Different Businesses)

in this cluster we provide training programs to SMEs and entrepreneurs to help them better integrate technology into work for better business performance locally and globally. This training is delivered through our Business Boost Programs.
